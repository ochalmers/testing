FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to get started?

Most brands are live within 4–8 weeks. Initial setup includes model conversion, configuration setup, and integration. We offer white-glove onboarding for enterprise customers.

Do you work with our existing product data?

Yes. We accept CAD files (SolidWorks, Rhino, etc.), technical specs, and even 2D reference images. Our team handles the conversion to optimized 3D assets.

Can we use this for B2B and D2C?

Absolutely. The same 3D assets power both. Use CPQ integrations for wholesale and custom orders, and embed interactive viewers on your consumer site.

What about returns and support?

Customers who configure and see exactly what they're getting return less. We also provide dedicated support, documentation, and success managers for larger deployments.
